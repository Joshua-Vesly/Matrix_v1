About Rachel PG

Rachel PG for Girls is a secure and comfortable student accommodation just 260 meters (4 minutes walk) from Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University). We offer fully furnished single, double, and triple sharing rooms with attached and common bathrooms. Each floor has a spacious living room for a homely feel. Facilities include CCTV, 24/7 water, power backup on every floor, WiFi, laundry, and housekeeping on alternate days. Located opposite Once More CrossFit and near Chai 3:16. Limited occupancy ensures a peaceful stay. Student-only. No food. Curfew: 10 PM–6 AM.
